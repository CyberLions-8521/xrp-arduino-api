Constructor for an XRP Motor plugged into the microcontroller (brain).

Parameters
dir_pinThe pin used to control the direction of the motor. This is also called the "phase" pin, and is labeled as PH on the board.
speed_pinThe pin used to control the speed of the motor. This is also called the "enable" pin, and is labeled as EN on the board.
inverted(Optional) Whether or not the motor should be inverted. By default, the value of inverted is set to false.
